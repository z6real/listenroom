🎵 How to get audio working:

A screen picker dialog will appear (required by browsers even for audio-only).

Chrome / Edge / Brave: At the bottom of the dialog, enable the "Share system audio" checkbox before clicking Share. Without it, no audio is captured.

For a specific app: Sharing a browser tab works most reliably. For other apps, use system audio.

Mac: Needs BlackHole (free virtual driver). Windows: Works natively.
